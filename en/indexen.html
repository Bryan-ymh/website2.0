Security

Learn more about our cutting-edge security technologies to keep you and your money safe.

Hate to have your personal information leaked?

We use bank-level 256-bit encryption (AES256) to protect and secure all of your information, including personal data (like your social security number) and your transaction history.

How do we secure your information?

We use the latest Transport Layer Security protocol to protect your information when communicating with us.

Is there any fingerprint scan to ensure the right user login?

You have the option to use biometric recognition (such as a fingerprint or facial scan) to access your account and trade orders.

We constantly monitor the latest security technologies to make sure that our systems are cutting edge and comply with industry best practices.
